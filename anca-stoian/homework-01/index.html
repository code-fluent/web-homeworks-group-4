FIVE BEGINNER WITCHCRAFT ESSENTIALS:





SALT:

Used for purification, the construction of a circle, defence, keeping entities away from a place, a representation of the earth element.

ROSEMARY:

An amazing healing herb that can heal most issues. Used for cleansing in incense form. Also a herb you can use for practically any purpose in spells.

QUARTZ CRYSTAL:

Quartz is an extremely versatile crystal that can be programmed for any purpose. Mostly used to focus and direct energy.

CANDLES:
Rituals, spells, meditation, divination; you can do it all with candles. Candle magic is definitely a great place to start spellwork.
JOURNAL:
Doesn't matter if it's well organised, sloppy, a bullet jurnal or just a collection of notes. Keeping track of what you learn and what you do in witchcraft will help you grow so much.
